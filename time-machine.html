● STAGE 1: ORACLE BONE SCRIPT

甲骨文 — c. 1250 BCE

The earliest known form of Chinese writing, carved onto turtle shells and animal bones for divination purposes. These natural impressions formed the first systematic logic of Hanzi.

  • Medium: Turtle shell, Ox bone
